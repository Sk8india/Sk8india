    Rob Thomson is a New Zealand adventurer and long-distance skateboarder known for holding the Guinness World Record for the longest journey by skateboard. Here are some details about him and his incredible journey:

    Early Life and Background

  • Rob Thomson was born and raised in New Zealand.
  • He has a background in outdoor activities and adventure sports, which fueled his passion for travel and exploration.

    • The Journey

  • Thomson's record-breaking journey began in July 2007 and concluded in September 2008.
  • He traveled a total distance of 12,159 kilometers (7,555 miles) on his skateboard.
  • His journey spanned multiple countries, starting in Switzerland and passing through Europe, the United States, and China before finishing in Shanghai
  • Thomson's mode of travel was primarily by skateboard, but his journey also included some stretches covered by other means when necessary.

    • Challenges and Experiences

  • Thomson faced numerous challenges, including rough terrain, adverse weather conditions, and the physical toll of long-distance skateboarding.
  • Despite these obstacles, he persevered, often relying on the hospitality and generosity of people he met along the way.
  • He documented his journey extensively, sharing his experiences, photos, and stories on his blog and through various media outlets.

    • Recognition and Legacy

  • In 2008, Rob Thomson's journey was officially recognized by Guinness World Records as the longest journey by skateboard.
  • His achievement brought attention to the possibilities of long-distance skateboarding and inspired many in the skateboarding and adventure communities.
  • Thomson's journey is celebrated not only for its physical and logistical challenges but also for the spirit of adventure and exploration it embodies.

    • Life After the Journey

  • Following his record-breaking journey, Rob Thomson has continued to be involved in adventure sports and outdoor activities.
  • He remains an inspirational figure for those who seek to push the boundaries of conventional travel and explore the world in unique ways.
